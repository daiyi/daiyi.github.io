Software Engineer, re-frame. Berlin, DE

Frontend Engineer, Mesosphere. San Francisco, CA

Building the human interface for DC/OS (the Datacenter Operating System running on top of Apache Mesos), with the goal of visually abstracting datacenter management to an experience and ease reminiscent of installing apps on phones.

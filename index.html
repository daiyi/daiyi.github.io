I can do a little bit of everything, and if I haven't yet, I can probably figure it out.

Professionally, I like to build tools for people who make big things together (devtools for systems engineers; collaborative writing & co-authorship). Employers are most satisfied when I'm working at the intersection of software, design, UX, product, and research (a nightmareish 5-way intersection which would be better as a roundabout). I also have experience with writing, illustration, and teaching adults & children. But also, the world is big and I'd like to see what's out there. Show me what you've got.

I love to climb rocks big and small. In the alpine: adventure, teamwork/communication, persisting, the importance of eating continuously. On the boulders: precision, strategy, performance, the importance of rest & recovery. It's a form of self-expression, a lifestyle of collecting deep wells of tacit knowledge and self-understanding, and a way to connect with nature & fellow humans. I owe a lot to climbing even when I am too messed up to climb. I'm grateful for all the people I've met along the way.

See you out there. ✨
